What these figures are based on

These figures are based on the current information in your account and the data provided by your training provider which is used to calculate funding.

Included in this forecast

  • opening balance from current month
  • monthly levy funds based on your last 12 months average levy paid
  • cost of training for apprentices who have started
  • cost of training for apprentices who are approved and due to start
  • cost of training for apprentices that you have estimated
  • cost of training for apprentices that you have paid on behalf of another employer
  • cost of any co-investment due to your training providers
  • expired funds
  • Your annaul transfer allowance

Not included in this forecast

  • apprentices who are not approved in your account
  • paused apprentices
  • stopped apprentices

Quirks

  • If an apprentice has started but not been paid we assume that the following month they will be, this would include all outstanding payments.
  • Your transfer allowance is calculated on the last financial year and reset in the following month of May.
  • We only show the co-investment due from you and not the 90% paid by the government. Neither figure affects your funding balance.
  • The current months estimate may change depending on if the current month's payments in and out have happened. More information

Guarantee

This tool is designed to illustrate what your funds may look like based on the apprenticeships currently in your apprenticeship service account and the funding band maximum for the estimated apprenticeships you have added, using this estimator.

The actual amount of funds available is likely to vary if there are any changes made to your apprenticeship arrangements, including, for example, changes in the number of apprentices employed, or the cost and duration of their training.

You should therefore not rely solely on the information provided by this tool when making decisions about the transfer of funds.
