Allowing your tax details to be used

When you enter your Government Gateway details you'll be asked to ‘grant authority’ to allow your tax details to be used.

This means you allow HMRC to send the Skills Funding Agency details of the levy that you've paid - no other payroll information will be shared.

By continuing you agree to the following:

I have authority to add PAYE schemes to this account.

I understand that all account users will be able to see levy funds from any PAYE schemes I add.

All PAYE schemes I add are owned and operated by an organisation that'll use this account, or owned and operated by a connected organisation.

Agree and continue
