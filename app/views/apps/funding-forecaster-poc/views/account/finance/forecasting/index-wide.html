What do the figures mean?

The ‘transfer allowance’ column shows 10% of your total levy funds taken from the previous tax year.

The ‘actual cost of training’ column shows the actual cost of the apprenticeships that you’ve selected to fund with you transfer funds.

The ‘transfer allowance remaining’ column shows the difference between the transfer allowance and the apprentices you’ve already selected to fund.
