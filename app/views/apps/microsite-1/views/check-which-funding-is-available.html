Check which funding is available

You can get help from the government to pay for apprenticeship training.

The amount you get depends on whether you pay the apprenticeship levy or not. You pay the apprenticeship levy if you’re an employer with a pay bill over £3 million each year.

How do I find out if my organisation pays the levy?

Your finance or pay roll department will be able to help you.
