How you will pay for your apprentices?

You can pay for apprenticeships in a number of ways.

If you pay the apprenticeship levy, you’ll be able to use your levy funds to pay for apprenticeship training. You’ll manage your apprentices and funds in your apprenticeship service account.

If you're an employer who does not pay the apprenticeship levy, the government will pay 90% of the total costs and you will pay 10%. This is called 'co-investment'.
