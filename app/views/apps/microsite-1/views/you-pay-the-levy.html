You pay the levy

If you’re an employer who pays the apprenticeship levy you will need to set up an account on the apprenticeship service so that you can:

  • receive levy funds for you to spend on apprenticeships
  • manage your apprentices
  • pay your training provider
  • stop or pause payments to your training provider

The government will also add a 10% top-up to the monthly funds you receive in your apprenticeship account. This will be credited to you monthly.
