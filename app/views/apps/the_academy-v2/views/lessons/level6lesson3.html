Can public sector companies be connected in the apprenticeship account?

Correct

Most public sector organisations can not be in the same account, the service will not allow it by default. To add a second public sector organisation you must contact ESFA to manually add it.

How much of a controlling stake does an organisation need in another to deem it a connected company

Correct

The rules around connected companies can be complex but as a rule of thumb if your organisation owns 51% or more shares in the organisation you are connected, likewise if the majority of control at a board level is by board members in your organisation you are connected. E.g. If your Chief Financial Officer sits on your board but also has another company he runs with a partner then that organisation despite not working directly with you would still be deemed a connected company by virtue that your CFO has a controlling stake in it.
