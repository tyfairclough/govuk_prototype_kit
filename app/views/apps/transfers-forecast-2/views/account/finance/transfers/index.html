Transfers

Employers who pay the apprenticeship levy can transfer up to 10% of their previous year's apprenticeship funds to other employers with an apprenticeship service account.

Employers who want to transfer funds first connect with a receiving employer through the apprenticeship service. Once a connection is made, a receiving employer can request transfer funds from a funding employer.

Before getting started, make sure you understand the rules for sending and receiving transfers.
