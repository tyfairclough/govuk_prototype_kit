Employing an apprentice

Use this step by step list to help you take on an apprentice:

  1. 1. Check funding available to you

    You can get help from the government to pay for apprenticeship training. There are a number of different ways, depending on your organisation.

    • Check which funding is available Levy funded Co-investment funded Transfer funded
    • Forecast my levy funding Completed
    • Get funding Completed

  2. 2. Choose your apprenticeships

    Find the right apprenticeship for your business and then find a training provider to deliver the training, who's either national or local to you. You can save your searches to refer back to later.

    • Find apprenticeship training and get costs
    • View your saved apprenticeships 0

  3. 3. Find training providers

    You can find training providers to provide training and save your chosen ones to a list.

    • Find training providers
    • View your saved training providers 0

  4. 4. Your apprenticeship service account

    You need an apprenticeship service account to recruit and manage payments for your apprentices.

    • Register for an apprenticeship service account
    • Sign in to your apprenticeship service account

  5. 5. Recruit apprentices

    You can advertise your apprenticeships and view current applications you have been sent.

    • Advertise an apprenticeship
    • View current applications

  6. 6. Review and approve apprentices

    Register or sign in to your apprenticeship service account to manage your apprentices.

    • Review and approve new apprentices
    • View your current apprentices
